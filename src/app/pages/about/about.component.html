nodeBucket

Nodebucket is an application for your most disorganized self. For all types of tasks, we have you covered! This tool can help you wherever you are to remember those todo's that you might otherwise have forgotten. Nodebucket is just the thing you need to stay organized and on task! Become who you have always wanted to be by getting everything you need to get done, done!

This application was specifically designed to be simplistic and efficient with plenty of features to meet all of your needs. From remembering to pay a bill or pick something up at the grocery store, Nodebucket has the versatility to do both, and much, much more. Easily create a new task with the convenient task bar and add it directly to your Todo list. Manage your tasks with the drag and drop feature to change the task order and to move tasks to the Done column. Any tasks that might accidentally be moved to another column can always be moved back at any time. Any task that you do not want on your list anymore can be permanently deleted if you so choose. Do not worry, you will be asked again if you really want to delete any of your tasks.
